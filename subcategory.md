# subcategory
From [[category]]
$\physics$
## Definition
$\cat{D}$ is a subcategory of $\cat{D}$ if $\ob(\cat{D}) \subset \ob(\cat{C}), \hom(\cat{D}) \subset \hom(\cat{C})$ and it is also a category (closed under composition, has identities for all objects).
