# dual space
From [[duality]]
$\physics$
## Definition
Consider [[vector space]] $V$, then:
Dual space of $V$ is $V' = \L(V, F)$.

## Properties
- It is a [[vector space]] with the same dimension
- [[dual basis]]
- If you have inner product: the natural isomorphism between $V$ and $V'$ is $v \mapsto \ev{v, .}$
