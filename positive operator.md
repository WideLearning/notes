# positive operator
From [[linear algebra]]
$\physics$
## Definition
A [[self-adjoint operator]] $T \in \L(V)$ is positive semi-definite iff $\forall v \in V: \ev{Tv, v} \geq 0$ and positive definite iff $\forall v \ne 0 \in V: \ev{Tv, v} > 0$.
