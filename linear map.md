# linear map
From [[linear algebra]]

## Definition
$$\begin{array}{c}
T \in \L(V, W)\\
\Longleftrightarrow\\
\begin{cases}
\forall u, v \in V\colon & T(u + v) = Tu + Tv\\
\forall v \in V, \lambda \in F\colon & T(\lambda v) = \lambda(Tv)
\end{cases}
\end{array}$$
$$\ker T = \{ v \mid v \in V, Tv = 0 \}$$
$$\im T = \{ Tv \mid v \in V \}$$
