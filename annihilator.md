# annihilator
From [[duality]]

## Definition
For $U \leqslant V$ it is
$$ U^{0}_{V} = \{ \varphi \in V' \mid \varphi(U) = \{ 0 \}\}$$

## Properties
- Annihilator is a [[vector space]] (and a subspace of $V'$)
- [[annihilator dimension]]
- In dot product spaces it is [[dual space]] of orthogonal complement
