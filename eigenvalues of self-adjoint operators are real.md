# eigenvalues of self-adjoint operators are real
From [[self-adjoint operator]]
$\physics$
## Statement
$$T = T^{*}, Tv = \lambda v, v \ne 0 \implies \lambda \in \R$$

## Proof
$$\lambda\norm{v}^2 = \ev{\lambda v, v} = \ev{Tv, v} = \ev{v, Tv} = \ev{v, \lambda v} = \bar \lambda \norm{v}^{2}$$
$$\lambda = \bar \lambda$$
