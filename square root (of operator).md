# square root (of operator)
From [[linear algebra]]
$\physics$
## Definition
$R = \sqrt{T}$ ff $R^{2} = T$.

## Properties
- [[square roots of positive operators]]
- $I + N$ has square root, where $N$ is [[nilpotent operator]]
- Invertible operators on $\mathbb{C}$ have square roots (from the above and [[Jordan form]])
